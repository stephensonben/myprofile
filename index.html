About Me

I have spent most of my life living in rural areas, with a few years in cities in between. I have a 2 year old daughter and what little free time she affords me I like to spend fishing and camping.

Before working in IT I did short stints as a (un)registered surveyor, a relief milker, worked in a sawmill, conducted road condition audits and worked in the mincing room of a knackery. That last job made me appreciate the value of a clean, airconditioned office.
