My thoughts on the test results


I think these tests are fairly good indication of my personality type and I agree with most of the results.
These results help to understand how others may perceive me and allow me to interact more effectively with them.

I have found that most good teams are a mixture of personality types. It’s important to always be respectful of the abilities and emotions of others and having an awareness of your own personality type can assist with this. By understanding how people like to learn, work and be communicated with you can more effectively work together.
